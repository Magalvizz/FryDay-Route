About Us

History

Fast Food Founded in 2014 by Miguel Damasceno, aiming to deliver quality and comfort food to customers, so that the experiences within the company would be incredible.

Over these 10 years we have gained many experiences, brightened up sad days and reconnected people to life once again. We value not only our food and customers, but also their well-being.

Mission

We want to improve your day, make you happier by connecting you and your loved ones to an unforgettable experience.

Having delicious moments while having fun is priceless, we value not only your experience, but your life.

Vision

We aim to grow our brand to become the biggest in our industry, so we can improve your day and present new experiences to those who have never had anything like it.

Values

A company's values are like the compass that guides its actions and decisions. They represent what the company considers important, defining its culture, ethics and behavior. These values, such as honesty, integrity, excellence, innovation and teamwork, serve as a guide for employees, ensuring that everyone is aligned with the company's mission and vision. They also help to build a solid reputation and strengthen relationships with clients, suppliers and the community.
